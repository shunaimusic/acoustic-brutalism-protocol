# RHA Protocol: Biological Integrity in Acoustic Brutalism
**Version:** 1.0.26  
**Architect:** shuNai  
**Status:** Implementation / Production Phase (Album: Virtue's Paradox)

## Overview
The RHA Protocol (Resonance, Hybridity, Agency) is a technical and philosophical framework designed to maintain **Biological Integrity** within high-density digital and architectural environments. Unlike traditional Acoustic Noir, which documents urban decay, RHA-compliant audio acts as a symbiotic interface between the human organism and "Brutalist" infrastructure.

## Core Pillars
### 1. Resonance (Biometric Sync)
Audio frequencies are engineered to achieve physical resonance with the listener’s autonomic nervous system. 
* **Implementation:** Utilization of low-frequency "Architectural Echoes" to ground the spatial perception.
* **Goal:** Preventing sensory fragmentation in liminal spaces.

### 2. Hybridity (The Organic Glitch)
Rejection of synthetic dissonance in favor of "Biological Artifacts."
* **Source Material:** Vocal fry, uncorrected rasp, respiratory rhythms, and micro-temporal stumbles (Biological Integrity).
* **Processing:** Preserving the "Biological Error" as a primary aesthetic carrier.

### 3. Agency (Adaptive Soundscapes)
Sound is treated as an active agent, not a static file. 
* **Concept:** The audio ecosystem responds to the listener's environmental stress, acting as a "Protective Exoskeleton" of sound.

## Technical Manifesto: "Virtue’s Paradox"
The upcoming LP *Virtue’s Paradox* (Release: May 7, 2026) serves as the primary data-set for the RHA Protocol. 
* **Anti-Pitch Correction:** Mandatory preservation of vocal friction.
* **Density vs. Volume:** Heaviness achieved through truth-density, not gain.
