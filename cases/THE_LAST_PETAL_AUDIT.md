# Case Study 01: "The Last Petal" — The High-Definition Vacuum

## 1. Objective
Technical implementation of the **Acoustic Noir** framework within the **RHA Protocol**. This experiment focuses on the "Memory Casket" effect—using minimalist sound design to simulate psychological stasis.

## 2. Technical Architecture
* **BPM Dynamics:** Variable (90 → 85 → 100 → 80 → 70). Intentional temporal decay to simulate the "Distorted Time" signature.
* **Frequency Management:** Focus on sub-audible minimalist textures. The low-frequency whisper acts as the central anchor, testing the limits of vocal intimacy vs. digital floor noise.
* **The Tsunami Effect:** A controlled structural transition from sub-minimalism to maximum-decibel saturation. Peak intensity achieved through high-contrast dynamics rather than layering.

## 3. Biological Integrity Audit
* **Quantization:** 0% (Unquantized live-piano and percussion).
* **Vocal Processing:** Zero pitch correction. Preservation of breath, rasp, and "human error" artifacts as a rejection of generative/sterile perfection.
* **Acoustic Signatures:** Integration of physical "ticking" mechanisms and room resonance to ground the track in the physical realm.
