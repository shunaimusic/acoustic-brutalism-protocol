# Case Study 12: "The Label" — A Post-Mortem of Rust

## Objective
Technical audit of the **Surgical Post-Mortem** of long-term domestic structures and the implementation of **Role Inversion Analysis** within the RHA Protocol. This case study documents the disintegration of a multi-decade narrative into raw, corrosive irony.

## Technical Architecture

* **Genre Engineering:** Post-Punk / Post-Grunge / Alternative Rock.
* **Vocal Profile:** * **Lead:** Male Weary Grit / Sharp Shouted Delivery.
    * **Delivery:** Transition from cynical, close-mic spoken-word verses to caustic, high-decibel outbursts in the choruses.
* **BPM & Dynamics:** * Verse: 88 BPM (Tense Post-Punk).
    * Chorus: 95 BPM (Post-Grunge Assault).
    * Outro: Sudden Tempo Collapse (Visceral Fade).
* **Sonic Blueprint:** Exclusive focus on thick distorted guitar layers, aggressive live drum patterns, and zero electronic instrumentation to emphasize "analog rust."

* **Vocal Processing:** Zero pitch/timing correction. Preservation of physical strain and vocal breaking during shouted sections to maintain **Biological Integrity**.
* **Acoustic Signatures:** "Digital Glow" room tone in verses shifting to a wide-angle, saturated sonic environment in the explosive segments.
