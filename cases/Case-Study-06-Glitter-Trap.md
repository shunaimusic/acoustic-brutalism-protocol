# Case Study 06: "Glitter Trap" — The Alpha Audit

**Objective:** Technical dissection of "Mental Structural Failure" within the RHA Protocol. This study maps the collapse of the suburban ego and the transition from curated control to devastating reality.

## Technical Architecture
* **Genre Engineering:** Suburban Rap / Aggressive Alternative Rock (Hybrid).
* **Vocal Profile:** * **Primary:** Male baritone, transitioning from a cynical, monotonous "Suburban Rap" delivery to high-intensity, gravelly rock vocals.
    * **Texture:** Heavy vocal fry and uncorrected "Human Error" signatures as markers of vanity's collapse.
* **BPM & Dynamics:** Bipolar structure. Starts with a steady, ironic rhythm, followed by a violent "Monolithic Explosion" (The Glitter Bomb effect).
* **Sonic Blueprint:** High-contrast dynamic shifts designed to simulate the disintegration of a filtered, artificial facade.

## Biological Integrity Audit
* **Quantization:** 0% (Temporal Friction). Absolute rejection of the digital grid during the rock transition to represent chaotic psychological breakdown.
* **Vocal Processing:** Zero Pitch Correction. Preservation of every sharp intake of breath and vocal instability as somatic markers of a "Pawn Status" realization.
* **Acoustic Signatures:** Analog grit and raw room resonance. Distorted heavy guitars colliding with unquantized electronic percussion to create "Structural Noise."

## Semantic Metadata
