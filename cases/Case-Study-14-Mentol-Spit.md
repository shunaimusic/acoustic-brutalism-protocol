# Case Study 14: "P.S. Mentol Spit" — The Terminal Post-Scriptum

## Objective
Technical and conceptual audit of the **Terminal Post-Scriptum** of the Virtue's Paradox cycle. This case study documents the definitive peak of the **Brutal Bap** sub-genre, utilizing a high-contrast fusion of 90s Boom-Bap and old-school Punk to execute a final **Surgical Audit** of social compliance.

* **Vocal Profile:** * **Verses:** Male Gritty Baritone / Sarcastic Street Flow (92 BPM).
