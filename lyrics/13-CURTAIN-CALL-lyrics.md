## Semantic Framework & Lore Analysis
* **The Evolution of the Djinn:** The transition from a trapped entity to a liberating, albeit destructive, force of truth.  
* **Dark Carnival Architecture:** Using dissonant Ska and Cabaret structures to score the fulfillment of soul-destroying desires.  
* **The Mirror Audit:** Forcing the listener to confront the "Monster or Victim" dichotomy within their own reflection.  
* **Anti-Festive Atmosphere:** Subverting upbeat genres (Ska) to represent the hollow reality of granted wishes.  
* **Terminal Catharsis:** The final release of the cycle where the truth is extracted, leaving only an empty bottle and an exhausted observer.
