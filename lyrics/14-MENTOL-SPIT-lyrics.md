## Semantic Framework & Lore Analysis
* **Terminal Post-Scriptum:** This track functions as the final audit of the cycle, purging the remains of societal expectations.
* **The Mentol Burn:** Representing the caustic, sharp "cooling" effect of truth that remains in the throat after the fire of rebellion.
* **Human Error vs. Social Smoothness:** The intentional preservation of vocal breaks and unquantized shifts as a defense of Biological Integrity.
* **The Sewer Rat Archetype:** Embracing the "stench" of reality as a mark of superior vitality compared to the sterile "gilded cage" of the office drone.
* **Still Breathing:** The definitive signature of survival. Despite the darkness, the Sovereign Victim remains functionally and spiritually present.
