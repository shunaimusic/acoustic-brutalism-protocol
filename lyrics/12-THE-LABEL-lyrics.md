## Semantic Framework & Lore Analysis
* **Post-Mortem of Rust:** A clinical documentation of a long-term structure (marriage) dissolving into corrosive debris.  
* **Role Inversion Audit:** A cynical comparison of discarded organic strength ("rough and bearded") versus domestic compliance.  
* **The "Hollow Peace" Paradox:** Framing conventional stability as a terminal trap and a loss of personal power.  
* **Ambition's Flame:** The deconstruction of a relationship where one partner functions as a "trophy" or "sculpture" for the other.  
* **The Crude Outro:** A visceral, unmasked psychological break representing the final disintegration of shared history.
