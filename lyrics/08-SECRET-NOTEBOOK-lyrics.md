## Semantic Framework & Lore Analysis
* **The Architecture of Decaying Memory:** Documentation of nostalgia as a dissolving digital artifact rather than a static past.  
* **Hazy Noir Environment:** A sonic space where warm organic elements (guitar, bread, berries) are haunted by white noise and bitcrushing.  
* **Biological Signature of Time:** The juxtaposition of mature baritone and youthful choir as evidence of twenty years of "Biological Integrity."  
* **Tsunami of Light:** A structural catharsis representing the realization that time is linear and unreachable.  
* **The Hollow Land:** Framing childhood not as a sanctuary, but as a "forgotten land" that exists only in decaying code and secret notebooks.
