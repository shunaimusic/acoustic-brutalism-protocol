## Semantic Framework & Lore Analysis
* **The Alpha Audit:** A clinical examination of the male ego and its structural failure under the pressure of vanity.  
* **The Glitter Trap:** The snare of filtered reality and the "shiny lure" of social status.  
* **Built to Break:** The architectural philosophy of creating structures that are designed for inevitable collapse (Acoustic Brutalism).  
* **Digital Safe Spaces vs. Analog Grit:** The conflict between the curated online persona and the visceral, physical reality of survival.  
* **The Victim Card Decree:** A study in how "survival" is used as a justification for parasitic empire-building.
