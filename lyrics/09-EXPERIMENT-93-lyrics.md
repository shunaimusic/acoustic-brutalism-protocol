## Semantic Framework & Lore Analysis
* **The Structural Debut of Brutal Bap:** The transition of the rhythm from a pulse into a high-velocity industrial assault.  
* **The Concrete Jungle vs. Halls of Privilege:** A clinical study of the friction between social classes and the masks required to navigate them.  
* **The Cool Facade Paradox:** A narrative deconstruction of the "hero" archetype to reveal the hidden, unquantized internal vacuum.  
* **Surgical Audit of Youth:** Recalibrating teenage crisis through the lens of the Shadow Auditor.  
* **Biological Integrity (Punk Signature):** Preservation of the raw, cracking vocal snarl as the primary evidence of authenticity.
