## Semantic Framework & Lore Analysis
* **The Architecture of Chaos:** Framing self-destructive loops as a structural load-bearing home.  
* **Addiction to Chaos:** A clinical audit of the "lust for wreckage" against the numbing effect of conventional healing.  
* **The Numbing Agent Paradox:** Deconstructing "peace" and "safety" as atmospheric voids that trigger the craving for destruction.  
* **Empires in Descent:** The central motif of finding identity through the observation of collapse.  
* **Biological Integrity (The Limit Signature):** Preserving the physical breaking of the voice as a document of exhaustion.
